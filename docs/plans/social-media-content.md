## Backlog (More Ideas)

### Problem posts (minimal text)
- "Swipe. Swipe. Swipe. Swipe." — fast montage of deleting
- "Delete. Delete. Delete. Delete." — text only version
- "10 minutes." — timer ticking, boring inbox management
- No text — just sped-up tedious Gmail triage

### Problem posts (more text)
- "Email apps are backwards. You swipe to delete what you don't want. But you don't want most of it."
- "Every email app asks the wrong question: what do you want to delete?"
- "Inbox zero shouldn't take 20 minutes."

### Solution posts
- "I don't delete emails anymore. I just don't keep them."
- "This app auto-archives everything you don't explicitly keep"
- "Email, but you only touch what matters"

## Status Key

- `idea` — just an idea
- `scripted` — script/copy written
- `recorded` — raw footage captured
- `edited` — video/graphic complete
- `posted` — live on platform
- `skip` — decided not to use

---

## Tools

- **Screen recording:** iPhone built-in + QuickTime for higher quality
- **Editing:** CapCut (video), Canva (graphics)
- **AI graphics:** Canva Magic Studio, Runway (for abstract visuals)
